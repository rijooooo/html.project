About Melanoma

Melanoma is a type of skin cancer that develops from the pigment-producing cells known as melanocytes. It is considered the most serious form of skin cancer and can spread to other parts of the body if not detected and treated early.

Early detection of melanoma is crucial for successful treatment. This website aims to assist in the early detection of melanoma through image analysis and machine learning.
